Who I am

Data science that lives where sport and story meet.

I have spent the last several years living at the crossroads of data science and competitive athletics. Right now I work as a Data Scientist for UVA's Sports Science and Analytics Collective, and as Student Director of Analytics for UVA Baseball, where I own everything from the data pipelines to the dashboards coaches lean on to shape lineups and game plans.

My favorite part is never the model on its own. It is the moment an insight becomes a decision. Whether I am building a pitch prediction tree, a 3D biomechanics tool, or a live efficiency dashboard, what I care about most is translating something complex into something a coach can act on in seconds. I like to go a step past the question I was handed, because the best answers usually live just beyond it.
